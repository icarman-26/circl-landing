Community Guidelines

Six things that keep Circl good for everyone.

1. Show up when you say you will.

Other people are counting on you. If something comes up, let us know — repeated no-shows mean removal.

2. Be kind, read the room.

Treat people the way you'd want to be treated walking into a room of new faces.

3. Keep it social, not transactional.

No selling, recruiting, or dating pitches. Just be a person.

4. Signals are private. Full stop.

Don't ask someone if they signaled you, and don't share yours.

5. One group, one vibe.

You're there for the activity. If someone's not your favorite person, that's fine — be decent for an hour and move on. Circl handles the rest over time.

6. We'll remove anyone who makes others feel unsafe.

No warnings needed for serious stuff. For smaller issues, we'll reach out first.

That's it. Show up, be cool, and let the rest take care of itself.
